Engineering Honors Ambassador

I am a member of the Engineering Honors Ambassador program at Texas A&M. We are a group of Engineering Honors students that represent the Honors program to prospective Texas A&M students, their parents, and other students here at A&M.

Over this course of this semester, I plan to volunteer by helping setup/takedown events like the EH Career Fair, work desk shifts at the Honors lounge, and talk to prospective students and their parents during campus visits. Our goal is to spread awareness of the honors program and help ensure that talented students find their way to us.


Undergraduate Teaching Assistant

Last semester, I started working under Professor Teresa Leyk as a Teaching Assistant for the Data Structures & Algorithms course. This was a perfect opportunity for me to contribute back to the Computer Science community by sharing the knowledge I learned with students who are taking the course.

Some of my responsibilities include:

  • Presenting lecture content to groups of 20 students during lab sections
  • Helping students with C++ and LaTeX homework during labs and office hours
  • Grading LaTeX homework and checking for cheating
  • Weekly meetings with the other TAs and Professor Leyk to discuss potential improvements to the curriculum
  • Incorporating TA and student feedback to inform improvements to MCQ and FRQ question bank
